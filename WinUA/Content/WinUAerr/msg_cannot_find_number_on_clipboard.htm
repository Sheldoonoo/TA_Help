Cannot Find Number on Clipboard

 

Cannot find number (<n>) on clipboard

 

Problem:

The Assign Variable step in a macro has been executed, but thenth number(noted in the error message) cannot be found on the clipboard.

 

Solution:

Make sure that you have included a step before theAssign Variablestep that copies the needed information to the clipboard. Also, check to make sure that your Assign Variable step has been correctly entered.
