Message 285

Slide reached ____ mm. Sample yielded. Run terminated.

Problem:

The sample has yielded, broken, or melted causing the drive shaft to reach the end of its limit (~25 mm travel). This will automatically end the experiment.

Solution:

One of the following should apply:

  1. No action is needed, if the sample was expected to yield.
  2. If the sample was not expected to yield, you can try decreasing the amplitude, autostrain, and static force; or try changing the temperature limits; or try increasing the sample stiffness by changing the physical sample dimensions to minimize yielding.
  3. The slide position operating range is 2 mm to 23 mm. Adjust the sample length as needed to maintain the slide position inside this range.
