Signal Nonconvergence

Sigmoidal baseline failed to converge.

 

Problem:

The sigmoidal baseline calculations did not converge on a constant baseline within ten iterations.

 

Solution:

Repeat the peak integration with different limits. Insure that more than 50% of the "apparent" peak area (the area between the curve and a straight baseline drawn from peak start to peak end) lies above the straight baseline for positive peaks or below the straight baseline for negative peaks.
