Message 990

Heater out of control.  The temperature derivative is zero while the power request is maximum.

Problem:

The thermocouple is not responding to the increased amount of power being delivered to the furnace.

Solution:

Try the following:

  1. If this error occurs at the start of a test, it could be the result of a delayed thermocouple response. Try starting the test again. If the error persists, go on to step 2.
  2. The problem may be an open or shorted thermocouple, or a thermocouple not installed correctly. Visually inspect all thermocouple assemblies and harnesses for proper installation. If the problem persists, please contact TA Instruments for service.
