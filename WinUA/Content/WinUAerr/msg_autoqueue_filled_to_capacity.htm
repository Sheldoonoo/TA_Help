Autoqueue Almost Full

The autoqueue is filled to more than 90 percent of its capacity. It is recommended that you process (autoanalyze) or remove some of the autoqueue records to avoid overflow of the autoqueue and subsequent loss of new records.

 

Problem:

You need to process (autoanalyze) the autoqueue. It has been accumulating records and has now reached at least 90 percent capacity. If the records are not processed and removed from the autoqueue to allow more space, incoming records will not be appended and will be lost.

 

Solution:

When the message appears to process the autoqueue (autoanalyze), select Yes and allow the records to be processed. Another way to autoanalyze is to select File/Run from the Autoqueue menu. The results will be added to the autoqueue log.
