Cannot Edit Macro Step

Cannot edit this macro step.

 

Problem:

The macro step selected in the Macro Edit window cannot be modified. You can edit steps that contain a text entry, insert parameter, cursor or mouse coordinate, checkbox or radio button selection, or color selection. You cannot, for instance, change a button selection.

 

Solution:

When you encounter a step that cannot be modified, you must delete, then recapture the steps as desired.
