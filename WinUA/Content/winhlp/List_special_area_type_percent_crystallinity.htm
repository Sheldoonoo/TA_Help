List Special Area Type - Percent Crystallinity

Percent crystallinity is available for DSC data files only. If you select Percent crystallinity from the drop-down list, the percent crystallinity (%Cr) is calculated using the following equation:

 

%Cr = area/standard heat x 100

 

When you click on Percent Crystallinity the standard heat entry field is displayed, allowing you to change the value, if desired.
