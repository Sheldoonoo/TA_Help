DMA Calibration Error - Sample too stiff

Could not continue with the calibration. Encountered error 'Sample too stiff'.

This error can occur during dynamic calibration if the sample is too thick. Use a steel sample that is between 0.12 mm (0.005 inch) and 2.0 mm (0.08 inch) thick.

 

Do not use the sample in question for compliance calibration procedures. Press Continue to retry the calibration with a different sample or Exit to abort the calibration.
