Status Message 291

Strain or displacement ramp ended by static force limit.

Problem:

This is a status message to let you know that the drive force being applied has reached its limit, which resulted in the termination of the displacement or strain ramp. There are three possible reasons for termination of strain and displacement ramps:

  1. The final strain or displacement value will be attained and the run will continue with the next segment in the run.
  2. The static force limit of 18 Newtons will be reached.  The segment will end but the run will continue with the next segment in the run.
  3. The sample will yield or break, allowing the slide to reach the end of travel limit.  The segment and run will end at this point.

This message simply indicates that the second condition (static force limit) was the reason for the segment terminating.

Solution:

No action is required if the data collected covered the necessary strain range.  If a higher strain level is required, use a sample that is less stiff.  This may be accomplished by using a sample with a smaller cross sectional area or a sample of a different type of material.
