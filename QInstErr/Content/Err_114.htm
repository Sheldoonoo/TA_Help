Message 114

Cannot air cool. Furnace must be in the open position.

Problem:

You tried to air cool the standard furnace while the furnace was closed. To prevent damage to the balance mechanism and other internal parts, the furnace must be open before you turn on the air cool.  

Solution:

Press the FURNACE key to open the furnace. Press the LOAD/UNLOAD key to unload the sample pan and then turn on air cool.
