Reordering Curves

You can reorder the sequence of overlay curves on a selected axis from the Curve Overlay Options  Curves Page as follows:

 

1. Right click on the Line Type field for the curve you wish to relocate. A pop-up menu is displayed.

2. Select the desired operation from the list: Move to Top, Move Up, Move Down, Move to Bottom, or Delete. The operation will be performed.

 

When you change the location, the selected line type, symbol, color, offset, etc. does not change.
