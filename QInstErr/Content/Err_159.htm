Message 159

Cell disconnected while method running. Run terminated.

Problem:

The cell lost connection while the experiment was in progress; therefore the run was stopped.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Remove the cell and reinsert it, making sure that the cell is fully inserted into the connectors.
  2. Restart your experiment.
  3. Call TA Instruments for service if the above steps do not solve the problem.
