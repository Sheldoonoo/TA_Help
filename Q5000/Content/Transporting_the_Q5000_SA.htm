Transporting the Q5000 SA

 

Important:  If you need to ship this instrument, you will need to contact TA Instruments' Service to properly package the instrument for shipment. Improper packaging will result in damage to the instrument.

If, however, you need to transport the Q5000 SA instrument yourself by vehicle, follow these instructions to pack up the instrument safely. It is very important to properly protect this precision instrument to guard against potential problems when the unit is unpacked and used again. These instructions are not needed when moving the instrument on a cart within the same building.

WARNING:  Have an assistant help you pack this unit.  Do not attempt to do this alone.

CAUTION:  Follow the directions on the packing instructions carefully when installing the shipping material.

Emptying the Humidity Chamber

You will need to drain the water from the Q5000 SA Humidity Chamber, if you need to pack up the instrument for shipment.

To drain the humidity chamber:

  1. Place a cup under the drain plug on the underside of the chamber.

  2. Pull out the drain plug and catch the water in the cup.

  3. Replace the plug when finished.

During removal, take care not to bend the hang-down wires or damage the hang-down loops.

  1. Press the UNLOAD button, and remove any sample pans from the balance.

  2. Remove the balance housing cover by removing the two screws in the back with a screwdriver. (See the figure below.) Retain the screws. Lift the cover off.

  2. Turn the instrument power switch to ON, if it is not already powered up.

Installing the Shipping Foam in the Balance

This procedure is performed with the balance faceplate and the sample and tare side covers already removed. If you have not removed them, refer to steps 1 through 6 in the procedure above.

CAUTION:  When repacking the balance, be careful not to damage the balance arm or hang-down loops.

  1. Using tweezers, insert the foam around the screw hole (see figure below) as follows:

    1. Gently compress the foam with the tweezers, being careful not to touch the balance.

  1. Replace the sample side cover and screw.

  2. Repeat the procedure to install the foam insert in the tare (right) side of the balance and replace the tare side cover and screw.

Installing the Q5000 SA Shipping Bracket

The shipping bracket (shown in the figure below right) is used to stabilize the humidity chamber during transport. It was removed when the instrument was unpacked and should be reinstalled as follows:

  1. Raise the humidity chamber all the way up using the humidity chamber up button .

  2. Use a 1/2-inch wrench to loosen the nut on the shipping bracket and raise the foot.

  3. Place the bracket under the humidity chamber, making sure that the plate at the top of the bracket is oriented on the four posts.

  4. Lower the foot until it rests on the platform surface.

  5. Tighten the nut to hold the bracket in place.

  6. Turn off the instrument's power switch, which is located on the back of the instrument and remove the power cord.

  7. Disconnect all other cables and lines attached to the back of the instrument. Place the cables and lines in a box to be shipped with the instrument.

Replacing the Shipping Pallet

NOTE:  The figure below right shows the Q5000 IR. The figure is used here because the mounting feet and base are the same for both types of instruments.

  1. If you have retained the shipping pallet, follow the next steps to mount the instrument. Have an assistant hold the front up while you remove the two mounting feet. Then move to the back and remove the stabilizer bar.

  2. Place the pallet on the floor. Using two people, lift the instrument onto the pallet.

  3. Use a 7/16-inch socket wrench to install the five hold-down bolts and washers on the bottom of the shipping pallet.

  4. If desired, you can wrap the instrument in a large plastic bag. Place the original box over the instrument, if it is available. The instrument is now ready for transport.
