Mathematical Operators

The following mathematical operations can be performed using the Navigator software:

+

Addition

-

Subtraction

*

Multiplication

/

Division

log

Natural logarithm

log10

Calculates the base 10 logarithm of the value.

exp

Calculates e to the given power.

exp10

Calculates 10 to the given power.

pow

Raises the first number to the second power.
