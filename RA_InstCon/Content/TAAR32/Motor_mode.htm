Motor Mode

 

The 'stiffer' the motor mode, the faster the transient response of the drive system. A faster transient response gives the ability to step change speed in a shorter time, and to better cope with difficult non-linear sample behavior. However, a fast transient response leads to the possibility of increased noise levels on the reported torque level.

Generally, it is best to leave the setting at 'auto' since this automatically changes the stiffness of the drive system depending on the apparent stiffness of the sample.
