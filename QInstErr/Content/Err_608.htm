Message 608

Heat exchanger flow error.

Problem:

One of the following situations could be causing the error.

  • There is air in the water lines.
  • The heat exchanger is not connected, is low on water, or is not functioning properly.
  • The water lines between the heat exchanger and instrument are kinked or obstructed.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Restart the instrument. Select Control/Prime Exchanger to run the exchanger a few minutes to remove all of the air. Select STOP to halt operations when the air is gone.
  2. Connect the heat exchanger (electrical connector and water lines).
  3. Check the water level in the water reservoir bottle and fill to at least 2/3 full or below the upper tube connection.
  4. Check the tubing between the heat exchanger and the instrument to make sure the lines are not kinked or bent. Straighten out any kinked tubing.
  5. Inspect the tubing for obstructions, such as algae buildup. If blockage is found, turn off the heat exchanger, disconnect and clean the tubing, then clean out the water reservoir. Refill the water reservoir bottle to at least 2/3 full and be sure to add conditioner to prevent further algae buildups. See the TGA online help for details.
  6. If the error persists, call TA Instruments for service.
