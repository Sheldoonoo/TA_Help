Script - Instrument Startup and Shutdown

Rheometer Start-up sequence

To ensure correct operation of your rheometer, it's important that you always follow the correct start-up and shut-down procedure.

  • Turn on the air supply to the rheometer and ensure it's set at the correct operating pressure. ( AR500 : 37.5 PSI, AR1000 : 30 PSI )
  • If you are using a Peltier system, you must ensure that it's fed with a supply of cooling water.
  • Remove the bearing clamp by holding it firmly with one hand while turning the knob on the draw rod anti-clockwise.
  • Lower the clamp from the rheometer and place to one side.
  • Switch on the computer and then the rheometer control box.
  • You can now start the Rheology Advantage™ software.
  • Click on the instrument status page button.
  • You should be able to see the values being continuously updated.
  • Now go ahead and attach a measuring geometry to the rheometer.

Rheometer Shut-down sequence

It is important to shut down the rheometer using the correct sequence.

  1. First, remove the measuring geometry by lifting the head.
  2. Detach the geometry from the rheometer clean it and also clean the rheometer.
  3. Fit the air bearing clamp to the rheometer.
  4. Close the Rheology Advantage™ software.
  5. Now switch off the power to the rheometer control box.
  6. Finally, turn off the water and air supply.
