Cleaning SDT Platinum Cups

 

The following is a recommended cleaning procedure for contaminated SDT platinum cups.

CAUTION:  This procedure involves the use of a caustic substance, consult the appropriate MSDS sheet before using sodium lithium tetraborate and nitric acid.

  1. Fill the soiled cups with sodium lithium tetraborate.
  2. Bake the cups at 1000 Â°C for one hour (not in the instrument).
  3. Add the fluxed cups to a 10 percent nitric acid solution in a beaker, with a stirring rod.  Place this beaker on a hot plate and warm the mixture for about ten minutes or until the flux dissolves.

WARNING:  Do not use hydrochloric acid.

  1. Pour off the nitric acid solution and flush the cups generously with water, then allow them to air dry.
