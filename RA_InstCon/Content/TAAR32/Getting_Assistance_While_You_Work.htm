The figure below shows the main window displayed when you start the Rheology Advantage Instrument Control program. Various ways to get information regarding the items on the window are shown.

Contents and Index

You can get Help the usual way. Just click Contents and Index in the Help menu. Click the Contents tab to scroll through a table of contents for the Help file. Click the Index tab to search for topics by using an index of Help subjects. Click on the Find tab to use full-text search and look for specific words or phrases.
