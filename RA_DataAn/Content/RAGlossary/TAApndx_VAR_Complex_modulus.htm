Complex Modulus

Description

The value reported is the magnitude of the complex modulus, G*. The modulus may be represented as a complex function under sinusoidal conditions, with real and imaginary components. Its magnitude is the oscillatory stress divided by the oscillatory strain.

Symbol 

|G*|

Units

 

Description

Abbreviation

newtons per square meter (pascals)

Pa

Related Variables

G* = G' + iG"   (i is the square root of -1)

|G*| = s0 / g0

G' = |G*| cos(d)

G" = |G*| sin(d)
