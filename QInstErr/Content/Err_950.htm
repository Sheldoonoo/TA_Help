Message 950

Force sensor indicating the tray is obstructed. Autosampler motion stopped.

Problem:

An error occurred while the Autosampler tray was moving. An obstruction seems to be impeding progress. The Autosampler motion is stopped.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Check to make sure that there is nothing that impedes the motion of the Autosampler tray.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
