Stress Correction of Parallel Plates Examples

 

Follow the steps below:

  1. First load the files listed below:
    • hand cream cone-0001f.rsl
    • hand cream plate-0001f.rsl
    • hand cream plate stress corrected-0001f sc.rsl

NOTE: If you wish, you can just load the first two files and let the software perform the stress correction by right clicking on the file hand cream plate-0001f.rsl in the Results list window and selecting the Stress correction item in the pop up menu.

  1. Plot all three results files onto the same graph.

Notice how the non-corrected data for the parallel plate shows an erroneous curve.
