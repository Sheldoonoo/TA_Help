Message 515

Failed to unload reference pan at the end of run.

Problem:

The Autosampler has been commanded to unload a pan from an empty cell reference position.  Remember that the Autosampler always assumes that the DSC cell is empty whenever you reset the Autosampler.

Solution:

  1. Stop the Autosampler sequence, if necessary. Close the DSC Instrument Control window and either reset or turn off the Autosampler.
  2. Then place a pan containing the reference material in the tray. Restart the Autosampler and reopen DSC Instrument Control.
  3. Reconfigure the system once the Autosampler has finished the validity check and retry your operation.
  4. Call TA Instruments for service, if the error persists.
