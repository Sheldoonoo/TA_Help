Viewing the Q5000 Information

 

Select View/TGA Information from the menu to open a window displaying the following selected information about your instrument. Choose the applicable instrument below.

Q5000 IR

When these functions are used appropriately they can provide valuable maintenance information for your instrument. View this information to determine when you will likely need to replace the halogen bulbs again and keep your instrument functioning at its best.

  • Time above 1000°C:  This field displays the amount of time that the instrument has been operating above 1000°C since the time was last reset.Click the Reset Time button toreset the time to zero (0), when you change the halogen bulbs. SeeReplacing the IR Bulbsfor instructions.

  • Time above 1100°C:  This field displays the amount of time that the instrument has been operating above 1100°C since the time was last reset. Click the Reset Time button toreset the time to zero (0), when you change the halogen bulbs. See Replacing the IR Bulbsfor instructions.

  • Run Time:  This field displays the total amount of run time for the instrument since the time was last reset. Click the Reset Time button to reset the run time to zero (0) when the halogen bulbs are replaced.

Q5000 SA

When these functions are used appropriately they can provide valuable maintenance information for your instrument. View this information to determine when you will likely need to replace the humidity sensors again and keep your instrument functioning at its best.

  • Time Sample Sensor Over 1000°C:  This field displays the amount of time that the instrument has been operating above 1000°C since the time was last reset.Click the Reset Time button toreset the time to zero (0), when you change the sample humidity sensor. SeeReplacing the IR Bulbsfor instructions.

  • Time Reference Sensor Over 1100°C:  This field displays the amount of time that the instrument has been operating above 1100°C since the time was last reset. Click the Reset Time button toreset the time to zero (0), when you change the reference humidity sensor. See Replacing the IR Bulbsfor instructions.

  • Run Time:  This field displays the total amount of run time for the instrument since the time was last reset. Click the Reset Time button to reset the run time to zero (0) when the humidity sensors are replaced.

Click Close when finished.
