DMA Calibration Error - Previous step not done

Could not continue with the calibration. Encountered error 'Previous step not done'.

When performing the calibration, the steps must be followed in sequence for proper calibration of the instrument.

 

If you have already skipped a step in the calibration process, exit the calibration and begin again. Do not skip any steps in the calibration process. Press Continue to retry the calibration or Exit to abort the calibration.
