Message 846

Lid opened.

This is an informational message to let you know that the AutoLid is opened on the DSC instrument. To close the lid, select Control/Lid/Close from the menu.

  • Select Control/Lid/Open to move the lid out of the way to its home position. This function will automatically occur at the end of a run.
  • It is strongly recommended that you NOT open the DSC cell at below ambient temperatures.
  • Select the Control/Lid/Close function to move the AutoLid over the cell and lower it down over the pans. This function will automatically occur when the run is started.
  • Select the Control/Lid/Cycle Lid function when you need to test the opening and closing of the AutoLid during an alignment procedure.
