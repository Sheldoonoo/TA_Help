Message 308

Furnace open. Calibration proceeding. Close furnace for best results.

Problem:

The furnace was either not closed or was closing when a calibration was started.

Closing the furnace protects the clamp and sample from air drafts that may increase the noise and uncertainty in the calibration results. This is especially important for instrument position, instrument force, and clamp mass calibrations. Dynamic calibration needs to have the furnace closed to properly compensate for the furnace's influences on the instrument's measurements. However, if your experiments are run with the furnace open, then perform the dynamic calibrations with the furnace open.

Solution:

Press the FURNACE key to close the furnace before starting the calibration.
