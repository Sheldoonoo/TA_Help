Message 683

Flange temperature limit exceeded.  Cannot start run.

Problem:

The flange is in an over temperature condition at the start of an experiment.  The experiment will not start until the flange temperature is below that maximum for the cell and cooler type currently installed.  

Solution:

Determine why the flange is over temperature.  Check that the cooler is working properly, and is properly attached to the cell.  Check that the cooler installed is what you have selected from the instrument preference page.
