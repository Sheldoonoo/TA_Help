Message 508

Failed to load reference pan during setup of run. Pan number xx.

Problem:

The Autosampler has been commanded to load a pan from an empty cell reference position. Remember that the Autosampler always assumes that the DSC cell is empty whenever you reset the Autosampler.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Stop the Autosampler sequence, if necessary. Close the DSC Instrument Control window and either reset or turn off the Autosampler. Then place a pan containing the reference material in the tray. Restart the Autosampler and reopen DSC Instrument Control.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
