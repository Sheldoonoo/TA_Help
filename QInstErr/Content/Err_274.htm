Message 274

Position calibration lost.

Problem:

The instrument was turned off, reset, or experienced a power failure while the drive was active and the old position calibration is no longer valid.

Solution:

Recalibrate the position using the position item in the calibration menu. To avoid this problem, always stop the motor and wait at least five seconds before turning the instrument off or resetting it. If the problem persists, call TA Instruments for service.
