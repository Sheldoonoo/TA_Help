Calibration Data Not Accepted

The instrument did not accept the calibration data. Retry the calibration.

This error can occur for several reasons:

  • An experiment was in progress when the calibration information was sent to the instrument.
  • The communication between the instrument and computer failed.
  • The instrument was powered off when the calibration was sent.
  • Calibration results were outside the allowable range.

Before performing the calibration again check and correct any of the problems mentioned above, then try the calibration again.
