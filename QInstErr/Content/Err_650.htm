Message 650

LNCS Fill Terminated.

Problem:

The fill process has been stopped. This may be caused by one of several situations: empty bulk tank, bulk tank valve closed, no fill line connected, or full tank detected before fill set point was reached.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following:

  1. Check to make sure the bulk tank valve is open. Open it if needed and repeat the fill process.
  2. Check the level of nitrogen in the bulk tank and refill if needed.
  3. Check to make sure the fill line has not been disconnected. Reconnect if needed and repeat the fill process.
  4. Make sure that the LNCS level transducer is properly calibrated to assure accurate level measurement and successful completion of a Fill. Contact TA Instruments for service, if the error persists.
