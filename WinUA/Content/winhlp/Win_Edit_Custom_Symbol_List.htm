Edit Custom Symbol List Window

Use this window to select the symbols that you want to apply to your curves. The symbols and font that you select here will be applied each time you apply the custom symbols to your graph.

 

Follow these steps to customize your symbol list:

 

1. Click on the first (furthest left) symbol in the Symbol List at the bottom of the window.

2. Click on the symbol from the top port of the window that you want to be used for the first instance of a symbol (on a curve or axis).

3. Click on the second symbol in the Symbol List, then click on the desired symbol for its position.

4. Repeat these steps until you have selected all of the symbol positions in the list.

5. Click on the Font button and select the font characteristics desired for the symbols.

6. Click on the OK button when completed.
