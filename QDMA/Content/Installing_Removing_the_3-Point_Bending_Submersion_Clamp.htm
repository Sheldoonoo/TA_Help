Installing the 3-Point Bending Submersion Clamp

To install the 3-point bending submersion clamp on the DMA, follow these steps (refer to figure for identification of the parts):

  1. Place the 15-mm clamp base into the submersion tank. See the figure to the right.

  2. Using a 3/32 hex wrench tighten the setscrews until they are finger tight.

  3. Using the same wrench, remove the top bar from the yoke of the moveable clamp.

  4. Raise the furnace.

  5. Insert the yoke portion of moveable clamp into the dovetail holder and align the clamp with the holder. See the figure below.

  1. Tighten the center setscrew with a 1/16 hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the setscrew.

  2. Install the assembled submersion tank and clamp base onto the mounting posts with the four screw holes centered over the four mounting posts. See the figure to the right. Take care not to damage the thermocouple.

  3. Seat the fixed clamp firmly onto all four of the mounting posts. Then tighten the screws with a 9/64 hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the attachment screws.

  4. Install the top bar of the moveable clamp onto the yoke using a 3/32 hex wrench. See the figure below.

  1. Tighten all screws with a torque wrench to 6 N.

  2. Make sure that the appropriate clamp type and mode are selected on the controller.

  3. Calibrate the clamp for clamp mass and clamp compliance (see Calibrating the DMA).

  4. Ensure that the long, flexible thermocouple is installed on the instrument. (Contact TA Instruments for service, if needed.)

NOTE:  In the submersion mode the long, flexible thermocouple becomes the sample thermocouple.

The clamp is now ready to be used. For information on mounting samples, click here Mounting samples. Adjust the long thermocouple so that it is 1 mm below and 1 mm to the side of the sample. When using the small 3-point bending clamps, the thermocouple should be as close as possible to the sample. The tip of the thermocouple must be completely submerged in the fluid.

Removing the 3-Point Bending Submersion Clamp

The following procedure is used to remove the 3-point bending clamp.

  1. Press DRIVE Float/Lock to lock the clamp in place.

  2. Carefully bend the long, flexible thermocouple out of the bath.

  3. Using a 3/32 hex wrench, remove the top bar from the moveable clamp.

  4. Loosen, but do not remove, the four immersion tank (fixed clamp) attachment bolts using a 9/64 hex wrench.

  5. Lift the submersion tank (fixed clamp) off the four supports.

  6. Loosen the setscrew on the moveable clamp and then remove the clamp by sliding it out of the dovetail holder.
