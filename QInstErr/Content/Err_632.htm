Message 632

Cell not covered at method start. Run continuing.

Problem:

The DSC AutoLid was not closed at the beginning of the run. The run sequence will continue.

Solution:

Always make sure that the lids are in place before beginning a run. The results from the run made while the cell was uncovered may not be as expected. The AutoLid assembly is disabled when the selected cooler type is quench cooler and when a pressure cell or differential photocalorimeter (DPC) is installed.
