Connecting the Air Cool Line

 

Air cooling can be initiated automatically at the end of an experiment to facilitate cool down. (Select the Post Test function for this operation.) Use the following steps to install the cooling gas line.

  1. Locate the Cooling Gas fitting on the rear of the TGA cabinet, marked with a 830 kPa gauge (120 psig) maximum warning label.

  2. Make sure your compressed lab air source is regulated to between 170 and 830 kPa gauge (25 and 120 psig) and is free of oil and water vapors.

  3. Connect a compressed lab air line to the Cooling Gas fitting.

NOTE:  Nitrogen may also be used as a cooling gas.
