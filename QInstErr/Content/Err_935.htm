Message 935

Probe calibration complete.  For best results it is highly recommended that a new oscillatory compliance calibration is performed.

Problem:

This message is to let you know that the probe calibration has been completed and it is best to perform an oscillatory compliance calibration procedure.

Solution:

Perform a oscillatory compliance calibration by selecting the option from the Calibrate menu and following the instructions. Click the Help button on the calibration window for assistance.
