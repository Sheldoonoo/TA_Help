Cannot Retrieve Record

 

Cannot retrieve record <database name and record ID>.

 

Problem:

The program was unable to retrieve the record specified in the message for one of the following reasons:

  • The user no longer has access rights to that record.

  • The record has been removed from the database. See information on archiving in the TA Instruments 21 CFR 11 documentation.

Solution:

Either obtain rights to the specified record from your 21 CFR 11 System Manager or restore the record in the database.
