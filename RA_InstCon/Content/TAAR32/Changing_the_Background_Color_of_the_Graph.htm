Changing the Background Color of the Graph

Follow the steps below to alter the background color used for the graph.

  1. Select Experiment from the menu.
  2. Click on Graph.
  3. Select Properties.
  4. Click on the General tab.
  5. Click on the Background Colour… button.
  6. Select the required color for your graph background.
  7. Click on the OK button (for the color selection screen.)
  8. Click on the OK button.
