Message 154

Cell not responding. Could not save data in cell memory.

Problem:

This message is caused by one of the following:

  • The cell was connected, but while information was being entered at the computer, the cell was disconnected.
  • You tried to enter a value that was stored in the cell and could not write to a cell because the cell memory has failed.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem.

  1. Remove the cell and reinsert it, making sure that the cell is fully inserted into the connectors.
  2. Resend the information.
  3. Call TA Instruments for service, if the above steps do not solve the problem.
