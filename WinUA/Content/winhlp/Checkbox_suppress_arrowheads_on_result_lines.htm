Checkbox Suppress Arrowheads on Result Lines

When Arrowheads are displayed any result lines are drawn with arrowheads pointing to the point(s) on the curve.

 

Check this box to always suppress the option and never display arrowheads on the result lines. This gives you the option of selecting Arrowheads from the Annotation popup menu, when desired.

 

Leave this box unchecked (default) to always draw arrowheads on the result lines.
