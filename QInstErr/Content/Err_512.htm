Message 512

The Autosampler failed to unload a pan from cell.

Problem:

This could be caused by one of several problems:

  • A pan that the Autosampler is attempting to load is missing from the cell. Possibly the pan was dropped.
  • The Autosampler needs the be calibrated.
  • The gripper fingers made poor electrical contact with the pan or did not optically sense the pan.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Calibrate the Autosampler. See the online help for detailed instructions.
  • Use alcohol on a Q-tip swab to carefully clean the gripper fingers and the optical lens.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
