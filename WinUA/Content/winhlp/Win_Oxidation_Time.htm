Oxidation Time Window

Use this window to enter the following two parameters:

 

  • Signal threshold, which is the point on the curve at which deviation from the baseline begins.

  • Oxidation gas start, which is the time when you started the use of oxygen in your experiment.

 

1. Enter the signal threshold value in W/g.

2. Enter the oxidation gas start time in minutes.

3. Click the OK button. The window will close and you can perform the rest of the Oxidation Time analysis.
