Changing the Graph's Background Color

Follow the steps below to change the background color used for the graph:

  1. Ensure that you have an active graph screen.
  2. Click on Graph from the menu.
  3. Click on the General tab.
  4. Click on the Background Colour… button.
  5. Select the required colour for your graph background.
  6. Click on the OK button (for the colour selection screen.)
  7. Click on the OK button.
