Complex Viscosity

Description

The value reported is the magnitude of the complex viscosity, h*. The viscosity may be represented as a complex function under sinusoidal conditions, with real and imaginary components. Its magnitude is the oscillatory stress divided by the oscillatory strain times the angular frequency.
