Finding the Zero (Datum) Point

Follow these steps to find the zero datum point:

  1. Ensure that the air is applied and rheometer switched on.
  2. Attach the required measuring geometry to the rheometer.
  3. Load the Geometry file into the software.
  4. Ensure that the instrument status page is active.
  5. Select Instrument from the software menu
  6. Click on the Gap entry
  7. Select the Zero gap option (toolbar button)
    Tip     You can access the above item from the button on the toolbar.
  8. Position the measuring geometry within 5 mm of the lower plate. (Use the up and down arrows.)  See also:  More information on positioning the geometry.
  9. Press the Continue button.
