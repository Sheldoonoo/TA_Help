DMA Calibration Error - Sample too weak

This error can occur during dynamic calibration if the sample is too thin and weak. Use a steel sample that is between 0.12 mm (0.005 inch) and 2.0 mm (0.08 inch) thick.

 

Do not use the sample in question for compliance calibration procedures. Press Continue to retry the calibration with a different sample or Exit to abort the calibration.
