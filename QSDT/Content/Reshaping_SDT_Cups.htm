Reshaping SDT Cups

 

The SDT cup reshaping tool consists of 3 partsâ€”an aluminum rod, a steel collar and a delrin die plate.  

The following is a recommended procedure for using the tool to reform distorted SDT platinum cups:

  1. Slide the collar onto the rod, moving it up to the large diameter section of the rod.
  2. Place the rod end (small end) into the ID of a distorted cup.  Some pre-forming may be required to get the rod into the cup.  Note that the collar should now be between the cup and the large diameter of the rod (the handle).
  3. Holding the die plate on a table, or similar flat surface, with the chamfered side of the central hole facing up, insert the rod/cup/collar assembly through the die plate, thus reforming the cup.  It helps to rotate the rod/cup assembly to facilitate reshaping.  Cups with distorted bottoms can be pressed flat against a table while still in the die plate.
  4. After the cup is reformed, remove the rod/cup/collar assembly from the die plate.  Slide the collar down the rod length to remove the reformed cup from the rod.
