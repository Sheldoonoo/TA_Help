Message 544

PCA experiments above 80°C require a non-standard quartz light guide.

Most PCA experiments are run isothermally.  The PCA is compatible with two Q Series DSC coolers—FACS and RCS.  Generally, PCA experiments are performed in the temperature range –50 to 80°C using the standard Extended Range Liquid Light Guide provided with the unit. A quartz light guide is available as an option and must be used for experiments above 80°C. The range with the quartz light guide is –50 to 250°C.
