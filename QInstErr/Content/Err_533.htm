Message 533

Autosampler arm tilt switch open.

Problem:

The tilt switch on the Autosampler arm is open. One of the following may be the cause:

  • Something has obstructed the arm while it was moving downward.
  • Mechanical adjustment is needed.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Check to make sure that there is nothing that impedes the motion of the Autosampler arm.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
