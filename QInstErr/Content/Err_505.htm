Message 505

The Autosampler failed to find the tray CW/CCW home position.

Problem:

This problem could be caused by one of several things:

  • The tray is binding on the cover.
  • The platen hub slipped, causing the flag to catch on the sensor.
  • There is a problem with the motor drive board.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Loosen the cover screws and adjust it so that the tray does not bind.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
