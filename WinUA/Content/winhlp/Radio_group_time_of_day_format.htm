Radio Buttons - Time of Day Format

Choose from one of the following ways to set up the data file time displayed by the program (e.g., displayed on the printed graph and in the parameter block information).

  • 24 Hours: Click on the 24-hour format to have the time based on a 24-hour day. This is sometimes called Military time. In this numbering scheme, after 12:00 noon, time will continue to advance sequentially1:00 pm is interpreted as 13:00.

  • 12 Hours: Click on the 12-hour format to have the time based on two 12-hour daily cyclesam and pm designations. In this numbering scheme, time is noted as either am (morning) or pm (after noon) in 12-hour cycles.
