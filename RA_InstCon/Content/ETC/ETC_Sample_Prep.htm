Guide to Sample Preparation

Follow the guidelines below when preparing solid samples for use with the ETC.

  • Prepare rectangular samples with uniform dimensions.
  • Recommended sample test length is 40 to 50 mm. Total sample length should be 12 mm longer.
    For example, if you choose a sample test length of 40 mm your total sample length should be 52 mm.
  • Recommended sample width is 10 to 13 mm
  • Sample thickness range is 0.5 to 5 mm.
