Message 143

Probe stuck to sample or movement is obstructed. Cannot move probe.

Problem:

The probe is stuck to the sample or its movement is somehow obstructed.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Clean the probe and stage as directed in the instrument manual to remove any sample residue.
  2. Check the probe and stage to make sure they were properly installed.
  3. Applying low (or no) force, the probe should move freely.
  4. Ensure that the instrument mode matches the probe type. If the problem persists, call TA Instruments Service.
