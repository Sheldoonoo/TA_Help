Message 1016

The balance housing temperature is not being controlled at the setpoint.  Either the setpoint temperature has not yet been achieved, or there is a problem with the balance heaters.  

Problem:

The Q5000 balance housing is not at the desired temperature set in the instrument control program. This may be because the temperature is still stabilizing/equilibrating after the instrument was powered on, or because the balance heaters are not working correctly.

Solution:

Allow the instrument to warm up for 30 minutes before using. If the error persists, call TA Instruments for service.
