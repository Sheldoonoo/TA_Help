TGA Platform Calibration

 

Platform calibration is used if the sample hang-down wire fails to pick up a sample pan during an automatic loading procedure. The platform sample tray (AutoTGA only) and sample arm must be adjusted so that the instrument can properly load and unload the sample pans.

There are two ways to adjust the TGA platform:

  • Through the instrument control software by accessing the Calibrate/Platform menu.

  • Through the instrument touch screen. Choose the link below that is appropriate for your instrument:

    • QNX/Platinum Touch Screen TGA Platform Calibration

    • Original Touch Screen TGA Platform Calibration

QNX/Platinum Touch Screen TGA Platform Calibration

 To adjust the platform through the TGA Q500 QNX/Platinum touch screen, touch the AUTOSAMPLER key, on the QNX/Platinum Control Menu Touch Screen to access the TGA Platform Calibration touch screen shown in the figure below.

NOTE:  The platform calibration procedure will vary slightly depending on whether your TGA has an Autosampler.

Calibrating the Platform Sample Tray (AutoTGA Only)

The following table describes the functions that apply to the calibration of the AutoTGA platform sample tray using the QNX/Platinum touch screen.

 

Auto Function

Description

Counter-Clockwise Arrow

AutoTGA Only:  Moves the sample tray counter-clockwise one position.

Clockwise Arrow

AutoTGA Only:  Moves the sample tray clockwise one position.

[Auto Number]

Displays the relative position of the tray. Used with the Counter-Clockwise and Clockwise buttons to adjust the tray position.

Test

Moves the sample tray the number of positions you selected and saves the information.

 

When you press the AUTOSAMPLER key, on the QNX/Platinum Control Menu Touch Screen, the platform will automatically move to the load position for pan #1. If a pan is not already in position #1 on the platform, place one in position #1.

Adjust the platform  as follows:

  1. Touch the Clockwise and/or Counter-Clockwise buttons to position the platform sample tray such that the inside of the bale of the sample pan is centered within the hang-down hook. (You will see the value of the current location change.)

  2. Press TEST when complete to move the platform incrementally to the new location and save the setting.

  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until the pan is at the desired position.

Original Touch Screen TGA Platform Calibration

To adjust the platform, touch the PLATFORM CAL key on the Control Menu touch screen to access the TGA Platform Calibration Menu. The keys shown in the figure here are displayed. A brief description of the function of each key is provided in the table below. The first row of buttons is only available on an Autosampler.

NOTE:  The platform calibration procedure will vary slightly depending on whether your TGA has an Autosampler.

 

Key Name

Description

CCW

AutoTGA Only:  Moves the sample tray counter-clockwise one position.

CW

AutoTGA Only:  Moves the sample tray clockwise one position.

[Number]

Displays the relative position of the tray. Used with the CCW, CW, LEFT, and RIGHT keys to adjust the tray position.

Left

Moves the sample arm to the left one position.

Right

Moves the sample arm to the right one position.

Test

Verifies the calibration adjustment you have made.

Save

Stores the calibration values.

Exit

Leaves the tray calibration touch screen menu.

Back to top

When you press PLATFORM CAL, the platform will automatically move to the load position for pan #1. If a pan is not already in position #1 on the platform, place one in position #1.

Use the navigational buttons to move the platform such that the inside of the bale of the sample pan is centered within the hang-down hook.

  1. Press one or more of the navigational buttons to position the platform. (You will see the value of the current location change.)

  2. Press TEST when complete to move the platform incrementally to the new location.

  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until the pan is at the desired position.

  4. Press SAVE, then TEST again occasionally during this process to move the platform back to home then back to the load position to test the position change.

  5. Once you are satisfied with the platform calibration, press SAVE.
