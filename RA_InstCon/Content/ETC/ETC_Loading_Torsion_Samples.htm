Loading Torsion Samples

 

When running experiments using solids in torsion, follow the guidelines below:

  • Move the rheometer head up to maximum position.
  • Install torsion clamps with appropriate spacer and clamp face into upper and lower clamp.
  • Setup geometry, calibrate clamp inertia, and zero length (Note: Advantage software will automatically zero the normal force prior to zeroing plates).
  • Load sample into upper sample holder and finger tighten clamp nuts.
  • Bring rheometer head down while aligning sample with bottom clamp and load sample into clamp. Make sure clamp nuts of upper clamp are facing front of instrument when lowering rheometer head.
  • Ensure sample is installed straight and follow the guidelines specified below.
