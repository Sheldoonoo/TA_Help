Message 534

Autosampler tray motion error

Problem:

The Autosampler has encountered a problem while attempting to move the sample tray into position.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Check to make sure the tray is properly seated and that there is nothing that impedes the motion of the tray.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
