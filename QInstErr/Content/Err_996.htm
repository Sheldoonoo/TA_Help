Message 996

The system detected a problem controlling humidity.  Run Terminated.

Problem:

There is a 2% flow difference between the primary and secondary Mass Flow sensors on either the dry or saturated gas supply lines.

Solution:

  • Check to ensure that there are calibration numbers in the tables for all humidity mass flow controllers.
  • If calibration is in place (as checked above), check the gas supply to make sure that ample gas is available and that the lines are open and flowing.
  • If the error persists, call TA Instruments for service.
