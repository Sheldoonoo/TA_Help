Stack Axes

When you select Stack Axes, the y-axes are adjusted so that the curves on the axes are stacked, one above the other, with no curves from one axis overlapping the curves of another axis. Click here for an example.

 

NOTE: This option cannot be used when one or more of the axes are displayed in log form.
