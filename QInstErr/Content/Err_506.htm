Message 506

The Autosampler failed to find the gripper open/close home position.

Problem:

This problem can occur if the finger retainer bracket is misaligned, if the kepnut on the gripper motor is too tight, or if there is dirt on the rotating gripper ring.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
