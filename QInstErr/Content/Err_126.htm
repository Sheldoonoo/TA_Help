Message 126

Probe or force not zeroed or calibrated. Cannot set force, measure or move probe.

Problem:

The probe or force was not zeroed or calibrated.

Solution:

Typically, this problem will occur because the probe is not calibrated. This happens whenever the instrument mode is changed. Calibrate and zero the probe each time you change the instrument mode.

The force may become uncalibrated when the values in the saved memory region are lost or the TMA software is revised. Perform the Force Calibration.
