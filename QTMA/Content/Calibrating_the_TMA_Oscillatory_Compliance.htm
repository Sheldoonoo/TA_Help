Calibrating the TMA Oscillatory Compliance

 

TMA oscillatory compliance calibration performs a 0.1N oscillation at 1.0 Hz without a sample and captures the Delta-L amplitude.  The collected data is analyzed by the instrument to produce a compliance factor in Âµm per N of oscillatory force.

To perform the calibration:

  1. Install desired probe and stage and perform the normal probe calibration. (The Film/Fiber probe does not require an oscillatory compliance calibration. The Dilatometer and Parallel Plate Rheometer probes are not supported in force oscillation mode).
  2. Select Calibrate/Compliance from the menu. The Oscillatory Compliance Calibration window is displayed.
  3. Clear the weight tray and sample stage, then click the Zero Probe button.
  4. Select the Calibrate button to start the oscillatory compliance calibration.

NOTE:  If you choose Abort, you must restart the calibration or select Exit to cancel the calibration.
