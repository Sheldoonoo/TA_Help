Bad Intersection

Onset or end not within limits.

 

Problem:

The onset or end tangent lines did not intersect within your selected transition start and stop limits.

 

Solution:

Reposition your analysis limits or manually adjust your analysis tangent lines so that they intersect within the start and stop limits (i.e., widen your limits so that they cover more baseline).

 

NOTE: ForStep TransitionorGlass Transitionanalysis, if the start or stop limit cannot be moved back far enough to establish a baseline, then consider usingWeight Change analysisorSignal Change analysis.
