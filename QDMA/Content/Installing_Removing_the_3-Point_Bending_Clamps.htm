Installing/Removing the 3-Point Bending Clamps

 

In order to use the 3-point bending clamp, you must first remove the existing clamp according to the instructions for that clamp. Then follow these instructions depending upon whether you are installing the large clamp, installing the small clamp, or removing either one of them.

See also:

3-Point Bending Clamps

Installing the Large Clamps

To install the 20 or 50 mm 3-point bending clamp on the DMA, follow these steps (refer to figures for identification of the parts).

  1. Insert the 3-point bending moveable clamp into the dovetail holder and align the clamp with the holder.

  2. Tighten the setscrew with a hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the setscrew.

  3. Install the large 3-point bending fixed clamp by lowering it onto the mounting posts, with the four screw holes centered over the four mounting posts.  Take care not to damage the thermocouple.

  4. Seat the fixed clamp firmly onto all four of the mounting posts.  Then tighten the screws with a hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the attachment screws.

  5. Make sure that the appropriate clamp type and mode are selected on the controller.

  6. Calibrate the clamp for clamp mass and clamp compliance (see Calibrating the DMA).

  7. Position the thermocouple so that it is close to, but not touching, the sample.

Installing the Small Clamps

To install the 5, 10, or 15 mm 3-point bending clamps on the DMA, follow these steps (refer to figure for identification of the parts):

  1. Raise the furnace.

  2. Insert the 3-point bending moveable clamp into the dovetail holder and align the clamp with the holder.

  3. Tighten the setscrew with a hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the setscrew.

  4. Install the 5, 10, or 15 mm base onto the 3-point bending fixed clamp.  (This step can be done before or after mounting the fixed clamp.)

  5. Slide the fixed clamp into position, then  lower it onto the mounting posts with the four screw holes centered over the four mounting posts.  Take care not to damage the thermocouple.

  6. Seat the fixed clamp firmly onto all four of the mounting posts.  Then tighten the screws with a hex wrench.  Do not over tighten the attachment screws.

  7. Make sure that the appropriate clamp type and mode are selected on the controller.

  8. Calibrate the clamp for clamp mass and clamp compliance (see Calibrating the DMA).

  9. Position the thermocouple so that it is close to, but not touching, the sample.

Removing the 3-Point Bending Clamp

The following procedure is used to remove the 3-point bending clamp.

  1. Press DRIVE Float/Lock key to lock the clamp in place.

  2. Loosen, but do not remove, the four fixed clamp attachment bolts using a hex wrench.

  3. Lift the fixed clamp off the four supports.  It may be necessary to raise the moveable clamp to its upper position before the fixed clamp can be removed.

  4. Loosen the setscrew on the moveable clamp and then remove the clamp by sliding it out of the dovetail holder.
