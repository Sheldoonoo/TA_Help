Macro Delay Window

Use the Macro Delay window to insert a time delay between two macro steps.

 

Follow these steps to insert a Delay step into your macro:

 

1. Open the desired macro.

2. Select the desired step position for the Delay macro step.

3. Select Delay from the Insert menu found on the Macro Editor window.

4. Enter the time, in seconds, that you want the macro to pause before the next step is executed.

5. Select OK.
