Message 507

Failed to close lid and (or) park Autosampler.

Problem:

The Autosampler has failed to close the lid or assume the park position. This can occur if the auto lid fails to trigger the closed sensor/limit switch or if the Autosampler failed to successfully complete the park sequence.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
