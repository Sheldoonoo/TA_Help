Shear rate

Description

The shear strain rate, i.e., the rate of change of shear strain with respect to time, calculated from the velocity and the measuring system shear rate factor.

Symbol 

 (gamma dot)

Units

 

Description

Abbreviation

reciprocal seconds

1 / s

1 / sec

Related Variables

Shear rate = Velocity x Shear rate factor

Shear rate = d.strain / d.time
