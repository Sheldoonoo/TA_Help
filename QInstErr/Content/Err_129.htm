Message 129

Isostrain deflection out of range. Change percent deflection.

Problem:

The LVDT deflection or force required for Isostrain operation is outside of the normal range.

Solution:

Revise the Isostrain segment in the method (using the Method Editor functions) so that the deflection required will reside in the LVDT range (displacement change less than + 0.1 inch) and the force range (0 to 1.0 N).
