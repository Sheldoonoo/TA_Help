Message 224

Sample weight out of range. Cannot start run or tare.

Problem:

This message was caused by one or more of the following:

  • There is no SDT cup loaded on the sample beam.
  • The sample weight exceeds the SDT's weighing capacity.
  • The sample balance beam is not operating correctly.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Load an SDT cup onto the sample beam.
  2. Use a lighter sample or mechanically tare your sample using weights on the sample balance beam.
  3. Call TA Instruments for service.
