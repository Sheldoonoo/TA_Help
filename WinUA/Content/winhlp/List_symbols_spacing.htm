Symbol Spacing List

Select the spacing to be used between symbols on the curve from the drop-down list.

 

  • Coarse is used to space the symbols farthest apart on the curve.

  • Medium is used to space the symbols closer together on the curve.

  • Fine is used to space the symbols even closer together on the curve.

  • Every point is used to place a symbol at every data point on the curve. (This can sometimes lead to a solid line effect.)
