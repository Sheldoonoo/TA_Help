Message 510

Failed to open lid and (or) unload sample pan at the end of run.

Problem:

The Autosampler has failed to complete the open lid and unload sample operation. This can occur if the auto lid open limit switch was not activated or if the Autosampler fails to complete the load sequence.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • Check to make sure the AutoLid works freely.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
