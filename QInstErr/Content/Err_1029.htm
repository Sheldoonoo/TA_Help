Message 1029

No flow detected from CCS.  Please check that the unit is on and operating correctly.

Problem:

The instrument is not detecting any cooling flow from the DSC Circulator-Based Cooling System.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Check the power switch to make sure the unit is on.
  • Ensure that the supply hose is connected to the supply port and the return hose is connected to the return port. If the hoses are not matched correctly, the flow sensors will not operate as intended.
