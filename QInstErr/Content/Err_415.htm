Message 415

Bad temperature reading. PCU heater relay has been disabled.

Problem:

There is a problem with the temperature measurement electronics while the instrument is idle. Either the Tzero or the heater thermocouples have exceeded the instrument temperatures. The difference between the Tzero temperature and the flange temperature is too large. This could be caused by a blown fuse.

Solution:

Turn the instrument off and then back on. (Do not press the reset button.) If you still receive this error, replace the fuse in the instrument. If the error still persists, call TA Instruments for service.
