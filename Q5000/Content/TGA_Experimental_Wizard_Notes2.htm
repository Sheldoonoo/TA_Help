Entering Notes and Gas Settings Using the Wizard

 

Use this dialog to enter or select additional information to be stored with the selected file. These items cannot be changed once the experiment is in progress.

  1. Enter the name of the Operator conducting the thermal analysis experiment. Up to 25 characters may be entered.

  2. Select the Pan Type from the drop-down list of available pans. This is a very important step for accurate testing results.

  3. Enter any additional comments in the Extended Text field. This may involve information about the sample or experiment, such as sample treatment or data sampling parameters. Up to 500 alphanumeric characters may be entered (approximately 12 lines).

  4. Enter the Flow rate to be used for the Sample. Click here for more information Q5000 IR Purge Gases and Flow Rates orQ5000 SA Purge Gases and Flow Rates.

  5. Select the Balance gas from the drop-down list, either Gas #1 or Gas #2, and enter the corresponding Flow rate. To change the gas type, access Tools/Instrument Preferences.

  6. When you have finished setting up this information, click Next to proceed.
