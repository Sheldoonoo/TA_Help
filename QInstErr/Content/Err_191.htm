Message 191

Hi-Res® segment not valid in current mode. Cannot start run.

Problem:

You attempted to run a method that has a Hi-Res® ramp segment, but the instrument does not have the Hi-Res option.

Solution:

Remove the Hi-Res ramp segment from the method using the Method Editor functions or upgrade your instrument to obtain the TGA Hi-Res option.
