Message 117

Sample pan is already loaded. Cannot load another pan.

Problem:

  1. You attempted to load a sample pan when there was a pan already loaded on the balance.
  2. The proper pan type is not selected through the instrument control program. (E.g., aluminum pans were selected through the instrument control program and platinum pans were loaded).

Solution:

  1. Unload the first pan, and then load the pan you want to use.
  2. Select the proper pan type through the instrument control program.
  3. Retry the operation.
