Message 925

The second calibration weight value must be greater than ___ g and less than or equal to 100 g.

Problem:

This error applies to the TMA force calibration. When this error is displayed, the second weight value selected for the force calibration is less than the first weight used in the force calibration.

Solution:

Perform the weight calibration with the smaller weight as the first calibration point.  Then use larger weights for all subsequent points.
