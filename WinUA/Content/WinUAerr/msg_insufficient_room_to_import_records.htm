No Room to Import Records

Insufficient room to import autoqueue records.

 

Problem:

You need to process (autoanalyze) the autoqueue. It has been accumulating records and has now reached at least 90 percent capacity. There is not enough room to paste in another record. If the records are not processed and removed from the autoqueue to allow more space, incoming records will not be appended and will be lost.

 

Solution:

Try one of the following:

 

1. Delete several records from the autoqueue to make room for the one you want to paste. Then paste in the new record.

2. When the message appears to process the autoqueue (autoanalyze), select Yes and allow the records to be processed. Another way to autoanalyze is to select File/Run from the Autoqueue menu. The results will be added to the autoqueue log. Then copy and paste the desired record into the autoqueue.
