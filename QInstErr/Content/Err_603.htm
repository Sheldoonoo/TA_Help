Message 603

Heat exchanger, temperature switch open. Run stopped.

Problem:

The fan is not running on the heat exchanger and the water is not being cooled. There is a problem with the heat exchanger and the current run has stopped.

Solution:

  • Unplug the heat exchanger power cord from the back of the instrument, then plug it back in again and try the operation again.
  • If the problem persists, call TA Instruments for service.
