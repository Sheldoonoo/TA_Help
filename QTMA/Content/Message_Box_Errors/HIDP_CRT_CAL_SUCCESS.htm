Touch Screen Calibration Successful

Instrument touch screen is successfully calibrated.

This message is displayed after you have successfully calibrated the instrument touch screen. Click OK to exit.

 

Touch Screen Calibration Procedure:

You can calibrate the touch screen by selecting Tools/Instrument Preferences, then clicking on the Instrument Touch Screen page to perform the following steps:

  1. Calibrate the touch screen, if desired, by clicking the Calibrate button, then press the reset button on the back of the instrument.

  2. A series of prompts will be displayed. To continue with the calibration, follow the directions displayed. Reset the instrument as directed and follow the prompts using a non-sharp, pointed object (such as a blunt pencil or pen) to tap the touch screen at the indicated points.

  3. Click the OK button when finished
