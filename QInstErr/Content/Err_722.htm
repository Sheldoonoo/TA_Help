Message 722

Pan mass ratio < 0.75 or > 1.25.

Problem:

The pan you are using is either too light (less than 0.75 mg) or too heavy (greater than 1.25 mg). The ratio of the pan mass values you have entered for the sample and reference pans exceed the allowable range.

Solution:

Try the following:

  1. Check that you have entered pan mass for both the sample and reference pans.
  2. Use pans that are more closely matched in weight.
  3. Do not enable the pan mass options. NOTE:  The reference pan should not contain any reference material.
