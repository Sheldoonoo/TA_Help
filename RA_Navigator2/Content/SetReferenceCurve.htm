SetReferenceCurve

Indicates the curve to be used as the reference curve. A specific temperature or the lowest, highest or closest to the mean may be specified.

Syntax

SetReferenceCurve(<ReferenceTempType>, <Temperature>, <TempUnits>)

ReferenceTempType
 Explicit = 0
 Low = 1
 High = 2
 Mean = 3

TempUnits
 Celsius = 0
 Kelvin = 1
