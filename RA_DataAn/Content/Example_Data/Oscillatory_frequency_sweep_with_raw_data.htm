Oscillatory Frequency Sweep Example (with raw data)

 

Follow the steps below:

  1. First load the file listed below:
    • PDMS Extended frequency sweep-0001o.rsl

  1. Plot the results file onto a graph.
     

NOTE: The G’ and G" axes are locked, i.e., they will also scale exactly the same way. To enable this feature, select Miscellaneous… from the Graph menu and ensure that the option Lock related y-axes is selected.

This data has the ‘raw data’ (both the wave forms and precision try info) stored. You can display this information by first ensuring that the Point graph and Strain control items are selected in the View tab of the Options menu.


Now, when you click on an individual point on the graph, you will see the raw data on these two windows.
