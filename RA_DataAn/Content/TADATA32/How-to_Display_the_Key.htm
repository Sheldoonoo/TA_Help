How to Display the Key

Follow the steps below to display the program key:

  1. Ensure that you have an active graph.
  2. Select Graph from the menu.
  3. Click on Key.

Tip: You can also access the key from the button on the toolbar.

  1. Ensure that the Displayed option is selected .
  2. Make any changes required to the appearance of the key.
  3. Click the OK button.
