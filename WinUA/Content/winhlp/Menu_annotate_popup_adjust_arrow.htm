Adjust Arrow

Select this menu item to adjust the beginning and end points of the arrow as follows:

 

1. Position your cursor over the inset view window. Right click to display the pop-up menu. Click on Adjust Arrow. Markers will be displayed to indicate the beginning and end points for the arrow.

2. Use your mouse to drag-and-drop the beginning point of the arrow at the desired position. Repeat this process for the end point.

3. Move the cursor outside the inset view window and right click to display the pop-up menu.

4. Select Accept Limits. The arrow will be adjusted accordingly.
