Concentric Cylinder Measuring System

 

Concentric cylinder systems, or cup and bob, are most commonly used for samples of very low viscosity that cannot be retained in the gap of a cone or plate. They can more easily be used by less skilled operators as the gaps used are larger and the systems are less sensitive to overfilling problems.

There are several different types including:

  • Recessed end
  • Conical end
  • Double concentric

Double Gap Concentric Cylinder

A double gap concentric cylinder system is a special concentric cylinder that has double the surface area in contact with the sample and is therefore ideal for very low viscosity fluids (but not for particulate samples.).
