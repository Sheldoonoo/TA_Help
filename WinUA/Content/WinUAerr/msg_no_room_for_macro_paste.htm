Insufficient Room for Macro Steps

Insufficient room. Cannot paste macro steps.

 

Problem:

You have copied several macro steps and attempted to paste them into an existing macro; however, there is not enough room for all of the macro steps. (A maximum of 800 macro steps can be included in one macro.)

 

Solution:

Delete several macro steps and try the pasting operation again.

 

NOTE: You can avoid this message, and use more than 800 macro steps by calling other macros within a macro. To do this you can take a portion of a large macro and give these steps another macro name. Then insert a macro step that calls the larger macro and therefore conserve the number of steps in the original macro.
