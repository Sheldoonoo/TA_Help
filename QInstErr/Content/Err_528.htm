Message 528

Cell lid failed to open.

Problem:

You are trying to open the auto lid and it has failed to respond.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Check to make sure the AutoLid works freely. If the lid does not open freely, gently open the lid manually to check for mechanical obstructions, then touch the OPEN/CLOSE LID key on the touch screen.
  • Make sure that you have not set the temperature so high that the lid begins to soften and adhere to the cell. If that is the case, heat the cell again and repeat the open command.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
