Message 228

Cannot tare instrument. Weight signals unstable.

Problem:

The weight signals are determined to be unstable due to either noise on the signal or drift due to furnace temperature.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Make sure the SDT is in a stable environment.
  2. Wait until the SDT furnace is at a stable temperature and try to tare again.
  3. Call TA Instruments for service.
