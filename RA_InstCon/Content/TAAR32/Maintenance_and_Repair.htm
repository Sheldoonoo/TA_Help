Maintenance and Repair

 

Adjustment, replacement of parts, maintenance and repair should only be carried out by trained and skilled TA Instruments personnel only. The instrument should be disconnected from the mains before removal of the cover. The cover has a tamperproof seal and should only be removed by authorized personnel. Once the cover has been removed, live parts are accessible. Both live and neutral supplies are fused, therefore a failure of a single fuse could still leave some parts live. The instrument contains capacitors that may remain charged even after being disconnected from the mains supply. See Information and Warnings.

The only maintenance procedure that you should perform is the cleaning of the air filter. For more information click here Cleaning the Air Filter.
