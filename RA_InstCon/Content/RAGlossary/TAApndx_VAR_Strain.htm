Strain

Description

The shear strain, i.e, the relative deformation of the sample in shear, calculated from the displacement and the measuring system shear rate factor. In an oscillation experiment, the amplitude of the sinusoidal strain. The term shear is often omitted, since rotational rheometers operate only in this mode.

 

Symbol 

g   (gamma)  or g0 for the strain amplitude

Units

 

Description

SI

dimensionless

CGS

dimensionless

Related Variables

Strain = displacement X shear rate factor

% strain = strain X 100
