Message 118

Weight out of range. Cannot start run, unload, or tare.

Problem:

One of the following situations could be causing the message.

  1. There is no sample pan loaded on the balance.
  2. The tare pan is heavier than the sample pan (e.g., Using a 100 µL tare pan with a 50 µL sample pan.)
  3. The sample weight exceeds the TGA's weighing capacity.
  4. The sample weight exceeds the TGA selected weight range.
  5. The balance is not operating correctly.
  6. The tare pan has fallen off the balance hang-down wire.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Load a sample pan onto the balance.
  2. Load the same type and size pans on both the tare and sample sides.
  3. Use a lighter sample or mechanically tare your sample using weights in the tare pan.
  4. Select Instrument Preferences/TGA page, then select "Auto" for the Weight Range. (Applies to Q500, Q50 only.)
  5. Remove the tare pan cover and ensure that the tare pan is still on the hang-down wire.
  6. Call TA Instruments for service, if the problem persists.
