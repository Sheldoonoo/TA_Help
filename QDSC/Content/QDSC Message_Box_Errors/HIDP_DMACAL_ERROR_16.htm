DMA Calibration Error - Slide force too small

This error can occur during calibration if the drive shaft does not move smoothly during the force calibration. The cause may be one of the following:

  1. The weight used for force calibration is too large.
  2. There is a problem with the drive electronics or the motor.
  3. There is something obstructing the movement of the drive shaft.

Make sure that you have removed the fixed and moveable clamps and that the weight used for force calibration does not exceed 100 grams, then repeat the calibration.

 

If the error persists, call TA Instruments for service.
