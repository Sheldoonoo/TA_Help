Message 688

Presently in the humidity water fill mode.  Cannot start.

Problem:

The TGA Q5000SA is currently in the water fill mode, which is used when the humidity chamber is refilled with water. You cannot start the instrument while it is in this mode.

Solution:

Complete the fill process, then press the Apply button on the Humidity Fill Mode touchscreen. This will restore the normal operating mode. See the TGA Q5000 online help for more information.
