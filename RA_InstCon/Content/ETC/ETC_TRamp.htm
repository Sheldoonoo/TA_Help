Temperature Ramp Test on Solids in Torsion

The following guidelines will assist you in designing a temperature ramp test

  • Decide on temperature range for sample. If you are unsure of the temperature range for polymer of interest, consult reference tables on glass transition temperature and melting points of polymers.
  • Run stress sweep with torque as control variable to determine linear region . Linear region should be determined at lowest temperature in sweep as the linear region will get shorter with decreasing temperature.
  • Plot G’ versus Strain and pick a strain in the linear region.
  • Select frequency of interest - 1 Hz (6.28 rad/s) standard for finding transitions in materials.
  • Select ramp rate - should be between 1 and 5°C/min.
  • Program Temperature Ramp test.
