Message 664

Must change the sample gas to gas 2 before selecting gas 1 as outside purge.

Problem:  

The gas flow rate to the sample (furnace) and balance must be individually controlled.  This can not be achieved when both the sample and balance are Gas #1 and Gas #1 is "outside purge."  Outside purge requires that the flow rate be controlled externally from the instrument, therefore the sample and balance gas cannot be both Gas #1 (as specified on the Notes page).  

Solution:  

Select the Experimental View/Notes page for your run.  Change the "Sample" gas to Gas #2 (Gas #1 is always the Balance Gas).  Then, open the Instrument Preferences/MFC Purge page and specify Gas #1 as outside purge, as Gas #2 as outside purge (if desired).
