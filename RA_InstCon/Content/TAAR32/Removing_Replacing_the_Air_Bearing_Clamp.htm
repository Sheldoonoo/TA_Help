Removing/Replacing the Air Bearing Clamp

 

 

 

CAUTION:  NEVER undo the air bearing until the air supply is connected and switched on.

Once the air supply is switched on, and you can hear the air through the rheometer, the clamp can be safely removed.

Removing the Clamp

To remove the clamp, firmly hold the clamp and unscrew the draw rod in the appropriate direction.

CAUTION: Never hold the draw rod and unscrew the clamp!

Replacing the Clamp

Replace the clamp exactly the same way. The air must not be switched off until the clamp is in place.
