Message 414

Power supply fan locked.

Problem:

The power supply fan cannot move and circulate air.

Solution:

  • Check the back of the instrument to make sure that no obstruction is physically preventing the fan from moving and blowing out air. Remove any obstruction, if present and repeat your operation.
  • If no obstruction is found and no air is blowing, the fan has failed.
  • If this error persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
