Paste At End (Autoqueue)

Use the Paste At End menu item to insert the record, which was previously copied to the clipboard (using either Cut or Copy), into the autoqueue at the end of the list of records.

 

1. Select Autoqueue from the main Macros menu to display the Autoqueue Window.

2. Cut or copy the desired record. (Use Select All to cut/copy all records.)

3. Select Edit/Paste At End from the menu. The record(s) will be copied from the clipboard to the end of the list of records in the autoqueue.
