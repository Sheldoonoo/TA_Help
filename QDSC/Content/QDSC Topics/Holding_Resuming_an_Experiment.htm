Holding/Resuming an Experiment

When an experiment is in progress you can temporarily suspend it and continue it later by using the following operations:

Holding an Experiment

Use Hold on the Control menu or on the tool bar to suspend an experiment, maintaining the current temperature conditions, until you resume or stop the method.   

Resuming an Experiment

Use Resume on the Control menu or on the tool bar to continue the method that was put on hold, starting with the suspended segment.
