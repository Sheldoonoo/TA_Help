Radio Buttons Horizontal

  • Left Radio Button (Horizontal Position): When the Left Horizontal radio button is selected, the legend/inset view is positioned on the graph using the left of the horizontal side of the box as the handle.

  • Center Radio Button (Horizontal Position): When the Center Horizontal radio button is selected, the legend/inset view is positioned on the graph using the center of the horizontal side of the box as the handle.

  • Right Radio Button (Horizontal Position): When the Right Horizontal radio button is selected, the legend/inset view is positioned on the graph using the right of the horizontal side of the box as the handle.
