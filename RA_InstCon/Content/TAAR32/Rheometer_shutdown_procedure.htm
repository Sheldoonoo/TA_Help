Shutting Down the Rheometer

 

Follow the instructions below to shut down the rheometer when needed.

  1. Raise the rheometer head.
  2. Detach the measuring geometry. See Attaching/Removing a Measuring Geometry for information.
  3. Clean the measuring geometry and the rheometer.
  4. Close Rheology Advantage.
  5. Power down the rheometer.
  6. Lock the bearing.
  7. Turn off the air line into the instrument.
  8. Turn off the water supply to the Peltier (if used.)
