Information and Warnings

Caution!

This instrument must only be connected to an earthed (grounded) power supply. If this instrument is to be used with an extension lead, the earth (ground) continuity must be maintained.

Usage Instructions

Before connecting the rheometer to auxiliary equipment, you must first disconnect the instrument from the mains supply and read the Installation chapter of the rheometer manual. Safety of the rheometer may be impaired if the instrument:

    •    Shows visible damage.
    •    Fails to perform the intended measurements.
    •    Has been incorrectly stored.
    •    Has been flooded with water.
    •    Has been subjected to severe transport stresses.

Nitrogen

Under certain operational conditions, nitrogen in both liquid and gaseous forms is present when using the rheometer.

WARNING:  POTENTIAL ASPHYXIANT  Liquid nitrogen can cause rapid suffocation without warning. Store and use in an area with adequate ventilation. Do not vent liquid nitrogen in confined spaces. Do not enter confined spaces where nitrogen gas may be present unless the area is well ventilated. Oxygen depletion sensors are sometimes utilized where liquid nitrogen is in use.
