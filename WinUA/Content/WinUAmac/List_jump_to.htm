Jump To List

Select the "Jump To" option you want to insert into your macro from one of the following:

 

  • Step– When you select this option, the macro will jump to a step number that you have entered in the Jump Step field.

     

  • Start– When you select this action, the macro will jump to its first step and begin running again.

     

  • End– When you select this action, the macro will jump to its last step and end.
