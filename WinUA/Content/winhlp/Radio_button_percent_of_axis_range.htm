Radio Button Percent of Axis Range

Click this radio button to position (offset) each curve on the axis by an amount equal to the percentage of the axis range entered when the Auto Offset button is selected.

 

This results in the offset of the curves from each other as follows:

  • No offset is applied to the first curve on an axis.

  • The second curve is offset by the amount equal to the percentage of the axis range entered when the Auto Offset button is selected.

  • The third curve is offset by an amount equal to twice the percentage of the axis range entered when the Auto Offset button is selected.

  • The fourth curve is offset by the amount equal to three times the percentage of the axis range entered when the Auto Offset button is selected.

  • And so on
