Selecting Logarithmic or Linear Scales

Follow the steps below to change between log or linear options.

  1. Select Experiment from the menu.
  2. Click on Graph.
  3. Select Log/Linear.
  4. A tick against an axis indicates you wish to use log scales.
  5. If you wish to use the default settings, click on the Set to defaults button.
  6. Click the OK button.

NOTE:  You can also right click on the graph over an axis to switch it between log and linear.
