Cleaning TGA  Sample Pans

 

The TGA platinum and alumina sample pans are designed to be reusable. However, they must be thoroughly cleaned between experiments. This is typically accomplished by "burn-off" of residue in a propane torch. In some cases, soaking the pans in an appropriate solvent provides another alternative. Care must be taken not to deform the pan and bail wire during cleaning or the TGA automatic pan pick-up process will not work.

CAUTION: The aluminum pans can not be cleaned with a propane torch.
