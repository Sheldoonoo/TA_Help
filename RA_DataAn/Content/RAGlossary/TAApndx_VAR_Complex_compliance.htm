Complex Compliance

Description

The value reported is the magnitude of the complex compliance, J*. The compliance may be represented as a complex function under sinusoidal conditions, with real and imaginary components. Its magnitude is the oscillatory strain divided by oscillatory stress.

Symbol 

|J*|

Units

 

Description

Abbreviation

Related Variables

J* = J' - i J" (i is the square root of -1)
