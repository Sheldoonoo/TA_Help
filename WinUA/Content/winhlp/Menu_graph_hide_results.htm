Hide Results

Click on Hide Results to check this option. Checking this item causes any results currently shown on the plot to be hidden from view, but not deleted.

 

To display the results again, uncheck the Hide Results option:

 

  • In a normal plot, unchecking this item causes any hidden results to be redrawn.

  • In an overlay plot, unchecking this item causes any results that are not hidden on the associated normal plot(s) to be redrawn on the overlay plot.
