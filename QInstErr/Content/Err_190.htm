Message 190

Sample pan load error. Pan #xx.

Problem:

One of the following situations could be causing the problem loading or unloading a sample either during LOAD, UNLOAD or TARE or at method start.

  1. The sample pan was not sitting in the tray position ready for loading.
  2. The tare pan is heavier than the sample pan (e.g., Using a 100 µL tare pan with a 50 µL sample pan.)
  3. The total weight on the balance exceeded capacity.
  4. The sample pan bail was not engaged by the hang-down wire.
  5. The TGA is not operating properly.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Place a sample pan on the sample platform.
  2. Load the same type and size pans on both the tare and sample sides.
  3. The balance's electronic weighing capacity ranges from -100 mg to 1200 mg. Make sure that the total weight of the sample and sample pan minus the weight of the tare and tare pan falls within this range.
  4. Align the sample hang-down wire and, if necessary, adjust the sample platform.
  5. Switch the range to 1000 mg or Auto, if the sample is over 200 mg.
  6. Call TA Instruments for service.
