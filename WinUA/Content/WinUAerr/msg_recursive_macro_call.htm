Macro Cannot Execute Itself

A macro cannot execute itself.

 

Problem:

During macro execution, the macro named has attempted to call itself (i.e.,a recursive macro), either directly or indirectly through another macro.

 

Solution:

Rearrange your macro so that a recursive macro call is not required. A sequence of macro steps, terminating in a jump step that has been set up to loop back to the first step in the sequence can often be substituted for a recursive macro call.
