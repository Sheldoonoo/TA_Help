Hints for Positioning the Geometry

 

Coaxial Cylinders

One way to set the gap is to position the bob just into the cup before letting the automatic system take over. This can take some time (due to the large gap between the bob and the base of the cup). To overcome this, set the Fine velocity high in the Instrument Options menu and run a 'coarse' gap set. When complete, do not move the head (say No to the prompt) and then re-run the zeroing with the Fine velocity set back to your standard setting.

Double Gap Systems

Move the head until the top of the inner measuring geometry is just level with the top of the cup.

Controlling the Descent Speed

Set the speed at which the rheometer head will move when you use the Up/Down buttons on the Zero Gap screen as follows:

  1. Select Options/Instrument from the menu.
  2. Click on the Gap tab.
  3. Enter the desired speed of the rheometer head in the Other velocity field.
