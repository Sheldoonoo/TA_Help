Message 400

Power supply air over temperature.

Problem:

The air temperature inside the PCU is too high. The PCU fan may have stopped working, the ventilation holes are blocked, or there is a problem with the temperature transducer inside the PCU.

Solution:

Check ventilation holes for dust buildup or items too close to the instrument that make block the air inlet vents on the side of the cabinet. Check the fan exhaust at the back of the instrument for blockage. Clear away any blockage found. If this message persists, despite clearing the ventilation and fan, contact TA Instruments for service.
