Message 1035

Selected Cooler, MCA70, not detected at method start. Cannot start run.

Problem:

The MCA70 cooler was selected on the Instrument Preferences/TMA Page, but the cooler was not detected when the experiment was started. The run will not begin.

Solution:

Verify that the MCA70 is correctly connected to the TMA and is switched on. Try to run your experiment again. If the error persists, call TA Instruments for service.
