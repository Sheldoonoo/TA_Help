PromptMessage

Displays a message that requires user acknowledgement before next step is executed (i.e., OK or Cancel).

Syntax

PromptMessage ("Example Message", 0)
the message will remain on the screen until acknowledged by the user.
0 indicates that the prompt will be OK only.

 

PromptMessage ("Example Message", 1)
the message will remain on the screen until acknowledged by the user.
1 indicates that the prompt will have the options OK and cancel.
