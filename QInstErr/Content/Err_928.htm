Message 928

Balance with/without weight failed.

Problem:

The calibration step that was just performed has not been completed successfully. The instrument failed to balance the probe on the stage.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Confirm that the force coil is plugged in correctly (polarity is correct).
  • Ensure that the probe can move freely, repeat the calibration step, and try this operation again.
  • If the problem persists, call TA Instrument for service.
