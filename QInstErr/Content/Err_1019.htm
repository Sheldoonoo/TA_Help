Message 1019

The humidity water reservoir level is low.  It was also selected through instrument preferences to stop the sequence when this occurs.  Please refill the reservoir in order to continue.

Problem:  

The humidity experiment has been stopped due to the low amount of water in the chamber.

Solution:

Refill the humidity chamber as directed below:

 

CAUTION:  Distilled water is required for the Q5000 SA humidity chamber to prevent mineral buildup. DO NOT add any conditioner (antifungal agent) to the distilled water.

  1. Using the touch screen on the instrument, select the Control Screen.
  2. Enable the Humidity Fill Mode using one of two methods:
    • Press the Auto Fill   button on the touch screen, or
    • Using the Control Command pull-down menu on the Control Screen, select:  "Humidity Fill Mode" then select Apply.  
  3. Wait until the message at the top of the screen says "Ready" before proceeding.

NOTE:  If the Control Status indicates the chamber is still "Full," do not add more water, even if you have run experiments since the last time the chamber was filled.

  1. Remove the rubber cap from the water fill fitting on the left side of the humidity chamber shown in the figure to the right.
  2. Using the syringe (see the figure below), slowly add distilled water to the humidity chamber until a beep is heard from the instrument. When the chamber is full, the Control Status on the display will change to "Full."

NOTE:  There is a time delay associated with the FULL sensor. It will take several seconds for the sensor to register that the chamber is full and to activate the display and beep. Therefore, fill the chamber slowly to avoid overfilling.

  1. Press Apply to disable Humidity Fill Mode. NOTE:  You will not be able to run subsequent humidity experiments if the instrument is still in the Fill Mode.
  2. Replace the rubber cap on the water fill fitting. NOTE:  You will not be able to obtain reasonable humidity results if this cap is not replaced.

NOTE:  You will need to allow sufficient time for the water to equilibrate to the temperature of interest before proceeding with an experiment. Because the water in the humidity chamber should be at temperature equilibrium, it is generally not desirable to add water during an experiment. The instrument cannot be placed in Fill Mode while experiments are running.
