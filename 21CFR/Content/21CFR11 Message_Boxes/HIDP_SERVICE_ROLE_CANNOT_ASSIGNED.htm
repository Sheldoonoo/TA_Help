Cannot Assign Service to Other Group

Members of the TA Service Group cannot be members of any other groups. Remove all Non-Service Groups, then try again.

A user that is assigned to the TA Service group cannot be assigned to any other group. That user can only perform functions accessible to a member of the service group.

You must either remove all other groups from the list or remove the TA Service group from the list if you wish to make this user a member of another group.
