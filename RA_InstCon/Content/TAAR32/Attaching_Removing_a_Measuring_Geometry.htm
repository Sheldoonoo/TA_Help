Attaching/Removing a Measuring Geometry

 

The figures below illustrate the method to be used when attaching or removing a geometry.



Attaching a Geometry

Follow these steps to attach a geometry:

  1. Turn on the air and remove the air-bearing clamp.
  2. Push the geometry up the drive shaft, hold while the draw rod is placed in the screw thread of the geometry, and screw it upwards. It should be screwed finger tight, but not forced.

Removing the Geometry

Remove the geometry by reversing the process.
