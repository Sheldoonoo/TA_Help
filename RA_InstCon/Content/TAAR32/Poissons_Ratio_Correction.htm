Poissons Ratio Correction

The correction is implemented for samples up to 3 mm thick. The software interpolates for thicker samples.

To activate the correction, click on the Poissons Ratio button. This will present you will a window with a check mark to enable the correction, and a series of materials with known values. You can also make additions to the list.

The default condition is no correction (i.e., as per Rheology Advantage version 1.) It is possible to apply the correction after the event and to version 1 files. To do this load the files into the Data Analysis package, table the data, and then modify the geometry template.

The correction has the effect of making the modulus independent of length. Without the correction, it is recommended that the samples be 40 to 50 mm in length. In the ABS example below you can see that the modulus without correction is sensitive to sample lengths below 40 mm. Applying the correction removes this dependence.
