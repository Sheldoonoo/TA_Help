Message 914

Verify that static force plus two times dynamic will not exceed 1.2N.

Problem:  

At the start of a run, a check is made on the static force and the maximum dynamic force amplitude applied during the method.  The total of these two parameters is greater than or equal to 1.2N.

Solution:  

You will need to ensure that this is not the case or clipping will occur on the force applied.  Either lower the amplitude of the dynamic segment or lower the static force applied.
