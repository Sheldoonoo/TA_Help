Choosing a Probe

 

The type of probe that you use is dependent upon the kind of testing information desired. The available probes and their associated tests are listed below.

Click on the name of the probe for more detailed information:

  • Penetration:  Softening point, Melting point
  • Expansion:  Expansion coefficient, Compression modulus, Tensile modulus, Glass transition
  • Macro Expansion:  Expansion coefficient, Compression modulus, Tensile modulus, Glass transition
  • Film/Fiber:  Tensile stress
  • Flexure:  Deflection temperature, Flexibility
  • Dilatometer:  Expansion coefficient
  • Parallel Plate:  Viscosity-temperature, Viscosity-time, Wall shear rate
  • Hemispherical:  Softening point
