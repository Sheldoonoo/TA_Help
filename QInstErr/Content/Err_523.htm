Message 523

The Autosampler failed to perform the automatic positional calibration of cell platforms.

Problem:

This error can occur for any of the following reasons:

  • The fiber optic amplifier has gone out of calibration.
  • The optic lens needs cleaning.
  • The cell calibration fixture needs cleaning or is cracked.

Solution:

Try the following:

  • Use alcohol on a Q-tip swab to carefully clean the optical lens and/or the cell calibration fixture.
  • Reset the Autosampler by touching the RESET key on the Autosampler Control Menu or by selecting Control/Autosampler/Reset on the instrument control program, then retry your operation.
  • If the problem persists, contact TA Instruments for service.
