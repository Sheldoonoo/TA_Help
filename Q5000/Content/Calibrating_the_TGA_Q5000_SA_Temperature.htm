Calibrating the TGA Q5000 SA Temperature

 

The temperature calibration for the Sorption Analyzer is a six-point calibration that is performed and verified by the factory during the manufacturing process. At installation your service representative verifies the factory calibration. This calibration measures the difference in temperature of the thermistor against a certified NIST calibrated thermometer. If the thermistor should need to be recalibrated for any reason, contact TA Instruments Service to schedule that calibration.
