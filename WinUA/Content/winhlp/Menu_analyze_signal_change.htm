Signal Change

Use the Signal Change option to determine the amount of change in the signal between two points on a curve. A horizontal line is drawn from your selected start point to a vertical line drawn from your selected stop point. The difference between these points is reported.

 

Follow these steps to determine the signal change:

 

1. Select Signal Change from the Analyze menu. Markers will be displayed on the graph.

2. Double click on the desired place on the curve to position the marker at the beginning of the region of the curve you want to analyze.

3. Double click on the desired place on the curve to position the next marker at the end of the region you want to analyze.

4. Right click to display the Analyze pop-up menu, then select Accept Limits (or press Enter).

 

The program calculates the signal change and displays the results in the signal units.
