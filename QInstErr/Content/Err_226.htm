Message 226

Sample or reference weight out of range. Cannot close furnace.

Problem:

This message was caused by one of the following.

  • There is no SDT cup loaded on the sample and/or reference balance beam(s).
  • The sample and/or reference material weight exceeds the SDT's weighing capacity.
  • The sample and/or reference balance beam(s) are not operating correctly.

Solution:

Try one or more of the following operations to solve the problem, then restart your experiment.

  1. Load a SDT cup on the sample and/or reference balance beam(s).
  2. Use a lighter sample and/or reference material.
  3. Call TA Instruments for service.
