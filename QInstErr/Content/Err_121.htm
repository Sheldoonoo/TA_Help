Message 121

Probe not zeroed. Cannot measure or move probe.

Problem:

When the system was reset or the mode selection changed, you did not zero the probe. The instrument cannot measure the sample or move the probe.

Solution:

Press ZERO LENGTH on the instrument keypad or touch screen every time that the system is reset, powered up, or when the mode selection is changed (e.g., when changing from TMA Automatic mode to the Automatic Film/Fiber mode).
