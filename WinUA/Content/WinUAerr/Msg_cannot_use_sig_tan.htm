Cannot Use Sigmoidal Tangents

Cannot use sigmoidal tangents on log axes.

 

Problem:

You attempted to perform an Integrate Peak/Sig Tangent analysis on a log axis. Sigmoidal tangents cannot be used on a log axis.

 

Solution:

Try one of the following:

 

1. Change the Y-axis signal type to normal (i.e., not log) when you select the signals to plot.

2.  Use the Linear or Sigmoidal Horizontal analyses for peak integration.
