Message 320

Bad dynamic calibration results. Results not saved.

Problem:

The lowest compliance, or thinnest sample, used in the dynamic calibration procedure was too weak. Use a steel sample that is between 0.12 mm (0.005 inch) and 2.0 mm (0.08 inch) thick.

Solution:

Run the dynamic calibration procedure again, but make sure that you do not use the weak sample to gather your data.
