Message 288

Oscillation amplitude is zero.  Check size and strain level.

Problem:

When performing DMA experiments, you can specify oscillation amplitude on the Procedure Page as either displacement (µm) or strain (%) for the Multifrequency Strain and Multistrain modes.

One or more of following conditions will produce this message by invalidating the conversion of strain in % to displacement in µm:

  1. You requested zero % strain.
  2. The sample geometry is unknown.
  3. One or more sample size entries are zero.

Solution:

Correcting the above conditions will resolve this problem.  If it is impossible to specify the sample geometry or input meaningful sample sizes, then you will need to switch to an amplitude table (rather than a stress table) for control.
