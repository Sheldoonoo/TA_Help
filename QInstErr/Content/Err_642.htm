Message 642

High volume pans cannot be used to obtain Heat Flow T4P.

Problem:

The high volume DSC pans cannot be used to obtain heat flow T4P.

Solution:

  • Either select a type of pan that is compatible with heat flow T4P. Available pan choices include aluminum, hermetic aluminum, gold, or  hermetic gold.

or

  • Select heat flow T1, which is compatible with the DSC high volume pans.
