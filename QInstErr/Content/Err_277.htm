Message 277

GCA pressure is too high. Gas flow is blocked. Run terminated.

Problem:

While you were running a method, the pressure rose to 8 pounds or more. The gas flow is blocked.

Solution:

Check for ice blockage in the GCA transfer line or the DMA furnace. Disconnect the transfer line and clear the blockage or allow the furnace to come to room temperature or higher to allow the ice to melt.
