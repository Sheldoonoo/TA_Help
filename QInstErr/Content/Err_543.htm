Message 543

Pressure cell requires T1 heat flow selection.

Problem:

When using the DSC pressure cell you have selected a heat flow other than the required T1 type.

Solution:

Select T1 heat flow on the Tools/Instrument Preference/DSC page, then  recalibrate for cell constant and temperature using the newly selected heat flow signal. If you do not recalibrate as directed, the heat flow results will be incorrectly calibrated.
