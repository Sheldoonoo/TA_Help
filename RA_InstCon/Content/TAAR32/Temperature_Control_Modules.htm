Temperature Control Modules

 

The TA Instruments provides several temperature control modules that extends the temperature range of the rheometer. A non-contacting induction coil is used to provide radio-frequency 'energy' that is absorbed by special geometries.

Temperature range:  -100°C to 400°C

Heating/cooling rates:  120°C/min

NOTE:  This product has been discontinued by TA Instruments (2004).
