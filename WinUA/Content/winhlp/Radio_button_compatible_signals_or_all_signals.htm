Radio Button Compatible Signals or All Signals

Choose the signals that you wish to use in the curve overlay graph:

 

  • Compatible Signals Only: Click this button to display the signals found in the selected files that are compatible with each other. For the signals to be compatible and overlaid on the same axis, they must contain the same units (e.g., nonreversing and reversing heat flow, flexural and storage modulus).

  • All Signals: Click this button to display all of the signals found in the selected files.
