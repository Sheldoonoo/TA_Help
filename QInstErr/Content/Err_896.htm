Message 896

Autosampler (TGA) calibration saved.

This is a status message recording that the AutoTGA (TGA Autosampler) calibration has been saved to the instrument.

To obtain accurate experimental results you should calibrate the instrument when you first install it.  For the best results, however, you should recalibrate periodically.

Two types of calibration are needed for the TGA:  temperature and weight calibration. Both calibration procedures are performed through the instrument control software. Access information on these calibrations through the TGA online help.
