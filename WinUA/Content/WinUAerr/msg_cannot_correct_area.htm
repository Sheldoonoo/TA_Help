Cannot Correct Peak Area

 

Cannot correct peak area for sample weight.

 

This message is displayed in the following two cases:

 

 If a peak on the signal "Wt corrected heat flow" is integrated with any weight option other than "Original" weight.

 

 If a peak on the signal "Wt corrected heat flow" is integrated with unnormalized area selected.

 

In either case the analysis cannot be performed as selected. Choose different analysis options.
