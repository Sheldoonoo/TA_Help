Selecting Appropriate Spacers

Follow the steps below to choose the appropriate spacer for the sample thickness and clamp face used:

  1. Measure sample thickness.
  2. Refer to appropriate chart for spacer selection.
  3. Round sample thickness to the nearest number on chart and select spacer.

Example:

Sample thickness = 0.66 mm.

0.66 is nearest to 0.65 on chart.

Round to 0.65 mm and look up this thickness in chart below.
