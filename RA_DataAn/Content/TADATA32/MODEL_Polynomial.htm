Polynomial

Although there are many ‘pre-defined’ models for describing the rheological behavior of a material, you may wish to define your own model. The polynomial equation allows you to do this.

The number of terms can be set between 2 and 8. The value k is 0 to (1-number of terms), thus if you select Terms to be 8, k is 0 to 7.

An alternative equation is the Straight Line.
