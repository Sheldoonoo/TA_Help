Message 275

GCA filled too full. Automatically reducing liquid to a safe level.

Problem:

During the manual filling process, the GCA was filled with too much liquid nitrogen.

Solution:

No action needed. The GCA will automatically turn on the heaters to boil off the excess liquid nitrogen until the level has receded to a safe level for operation. To avoid this problem, only fill the GCA three-quarters (75 percent) full, or to a total liquid weight of 71 pounds (32 kg).
