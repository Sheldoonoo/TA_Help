Message 724

A cooling ramp specified may exceed cooling capacity. Run continuing.

Problem:

The rate specified in a cooling ramp segment exceeds the cooling capacity of the current cooler at the final (lowest) temperature. The run will continue, but the ramp rate will be less than requested at the low end of the ramp.

Solution:

Make sure that you do not exceed the cooling capacity of the cooling accessory in use. See the online help for the applicable instrument for more details.
