Viscosity

Description

The 'resistance to flow' of a material. For a parallel plate measuring geometry, the shear rate and shear stress experienced by the sample vary with radial position, and the viscosity calculated is the "Newtonian equivalent", i.e. the assumption is made that the viscosity is independent of shear rate. The exact viscosity can be obtained by using the Stress correction transformation in the Rheology Advantage Data Module.
