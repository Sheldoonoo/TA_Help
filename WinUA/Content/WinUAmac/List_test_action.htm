List Test Action

Click on the arrow to display a drop-down list of available actions.

 

Actions for Variable Tests:

Jump to start– When you select this action, after the condition has been met, the macro will jump to its first step and begin running again.

Jump to end– When you select this action, after the condition has been met, the macro will jump to its last step and end.

Jump to step– list When you select this action, after condition has been met, the macro will jump to a step number that you have entered in the Step field.
